隐私政策

最后更新日期：2026 年 6 月 14 日

一、信息收集

本应用需要访问手机麦克风以采集音频信号，这是调音功能所必需的。

原始音频数据仅在设备本地实时处理，用于音高检测，不会上传至任何服务器。

本应用不收集、不存储、不传输任何个人身份信息（如姓名、邮箱、位置等）。

二、信息使用

音频数据仅用于实时音高检测，处理完成后立即丢弃，不写入磁盘，不进行录音保存。

应用偏好设置（如主题模式、布局选择、A4 基准频率）存储在设备本地 UserDefaults 中，仅用于恢复用户偏好，不包含任何个人身份信息。

自定义颜色覆盖数据同样存储在本地 UserDefaults，不会上传。

三、信息存储与安全

所有数据仅存储在您的 iPhone 本地，本应用不提供云端同步功能。

由于数据不上传，不存在服务器数据泄露风险。建议您通过 iOS 系统级备份（iCloud / 电脑备份）保护您的应用设置。

四、第三方服务

本应用不集成任何第三方分析 SDK、广告 SDK 或用户追踪 SDK。

本应用不使用 Cookies 或类似追踪技术。

本应用不包含第三方登录、社交分享等功能。

五、儿童隐私

本应用不收集任何用户的个人数据，因此对儿童和成人用户的数据处理方式完全相同。

如您对儿童隐私保护有任何疑问，请通过开发者渠道联系我们。

六、政策更新

本隐私政策可能会不时更新。更新后的政策将在本页面公布，并在应用更新时随版本发布。

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 14, 2026

1. Information Collection

This app requires access to the smartphone microphone to capture audio signals, which is necessary for the tuning function.

Raw audio data is processed in real time exclusively on the device locally for pitch detection and is not uploaded to any server.

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (such as name, email address, location, etc.).

2. Information Usage

Audio data is used solely for real-time pitch detection and is discarded immediately after processing — nothing is written to disk or saved as a recording.

App preferences (such as theme mode, layout selection, A4 reference frequency) are stored locally in UserDefaults on your device and are used only to restore your preferences; they contain no personally identifiable information.

Custom color override data is also stored locally in UserDefaults and is not uploaded.

3. Storage & Security

All data is stored only on your iPhone locally. The App does not provide cloud synchronization.

Since no data is uploaded, there is no risk of server-side data breaches. We recommend using iOS system-level backups (iCloud or computer backup) to protect your app settings.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or user tracking SDKs.

The App does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

The App does not include third-party login or social sharing features.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal data from any user; therefore, data handling is identical for children and adults.

If you have any questions about children's privacy, please contact us through the developer channel.

6. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updated policies will be published on this page and released with app updates.

We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest Privacy Policy.

四、第三者サービス

本アプリは第三者分析 SDK、広告 SDK、ユーザートラッキング SDK を一切統合していません。

本アプリは Cookie や類似の追跡技術を使用していません。

本アプリは第三者ログインやソーシャル共有機能を含みません。

五、子どものプライバシー

本アプリはいかなるユーザーの個人データも収集しないため、子どもと大人のデータ処理方法は完全に同一です。
